Historical Places

Emperors Palace

The place is also known as the palace of dreams. It is situated next to OR Tambo International airport. It offers a decadent accommodation hotels, health and beauty spa, a magnificent casino with gaming enjoyment, the finest dining options (Rosettas being my favourite) and spectacular entertainment choices.

JHB ZOO-LAKE

Fun and leisure. It is situated in Saxonwold next to Johannesburg Zoo, which forms part of the greater Zoo Lake area. It is a popular spot for picnicking, taking a leisurely boat ride "favourite part". It has its own information centre and has one of the popular African themed restaurants, Moyo.

Gold Reef City

Fun and games. A family friendly theme park. Gold Reef City is a premier entertainment destination situated in the south of Johannesburg. It offers a variety of entertainment which includes a thrilling theme park, a spectacular theatre featuring live productions and a variety of restaurants and fast foods for every taste.
